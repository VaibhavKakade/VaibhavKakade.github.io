Bhigwan backwaters

18 Sept 2018

YRC rides to bhigwan in monsoon. This was a muddy ride. As it had rained the previous night and at dawn, the roads were wet and muddy. Riding behind trucks, containers was a challenge. My helmet visor was immediately coverd with mud and I was not able to see. I had to open my helmet visor and then ride. But other challenge was, all that dust, rain, muck was going in my eyes.
